About What's Cooking?

Welcome to What's Cooking? - your personal culinary assistant!

This application allows you to upload images of ingredients, and we'll suggest delicious recipes based on what you have.

Our goal is to make cooking easier and more enjoyable by helping you discover new meals with the ingredients you already have.

Simply upload an image, and let us do the rest! We use advanced image recognition and recipe databases to provide you with the best possible suggestions.

Enjoy cooking!
